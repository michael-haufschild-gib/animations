The algorithm to choose the entry in the prize table that gets highlightes as jackpot prize is as follows:

1. Take the entry with the highest SC prize.
1.1 Take all entries that have an SC prize.
1.2 If there is at least one, the entry with the highest SC prize is the jackpot.
1.3 stop

2. Take the entry with the highest amount of Free Spins
2.1 Take all entries that have a Free Spins prize.
2.2 If there is at least one, the entry with the highest amount of free spins is the jackpot.
2.3 stop

3. Take the entry with the highest amount of GC
3.1 Take all entries that have a Free Spins prize.
3.2 If there is at least one, the entry with the highest amount of free spins is the jackpot.
3.3 stop
